## Summary

Develops and validates a smartwatch-based algorithm specifically for early detection of pulmonary infections. Uses heart rate, respiratory rate, and activity patterns to identify infection onset.

## Key Findings

- Smartwatch algorithm can detect pulmonary infections before clinical presentation
- Heart rate elevation and respiratory rate changes are early indicators
- Reduced physical activity complements physiological signals for detection
- Algorithm validated against clinical infection diagnosis
- Smartwatch-grade sensors provide sufficient signal quality for detection

## MAON Applicability

Extends MAON's illness detection capability specifically to respiratory infections. The combination of HR + respiratory rate + activity changes provides a targeted detection pattern. MAON collects all three signals from Apple Watch.
